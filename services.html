Group Coaching

Group Coaching

A small cohort of owners and operators meeting weekly inside a structured program. You get the benefit of peer learning, shared accountability, and curriculum that builds week over week, without the cost of a private engagement.

The format is simple. Thirty minutes of focused teaching on a Maxwell Leadership principle. Thirty minutes of discussion, application, and problem-solving with the group. Then you go do the work. The current cohort is running through John Maxwell's "Everyone Communicates, Few Connect," one of the most practical leadership books ever written for people who lead teams in high-pressure environments.

What is included
  • Weekly 60-minute group sessions
  • 30 minutes teaching plus 30 minutes of application and discussion
  • Maxwell Leadership curriculum with real-world application each week
  • Access to the peer group community between sessions
  • Available virtually or in-person depending on the group

Peer Leadership

Mastermind Events

Mastermind events are where things get serious. A small group of leaders working through a shared curriculum, holding each other accountable, and doing the kind of thinking that does not happen inside the four walls of your own business.

These are structured around Maxwell Leadership books and designed to run over an eight-week arc. Each week has a clear focus, a practical application, and an expectation that you actually go use what you learned before the next session. This is not a conversation club. It is a working group for operators who are committed to getting better.

What is included
  • Eight-week cohort format with structured weekly sessions
  • Maxwell Leadership curriculum as the backbone throughout
  • Peer accountability built into the format, not added as an afterthought
  • Real-world application exercises between each session
  • Limited enrollment to keep the group tight and the conversation honest

Behavioral Assessment

DISC Assessment & Debrief

Most people have heard of personality assessments. Most of those assessments sit in an email and never get used.

The DISC debrief at Upward Path is different because we spend time actually working through what your results mean for the way you lead, communicate, and make decisions. Not just "here is your profile." But here is why your techs are responding to you the way they are, here is why that conversation with your service advisor keeps going sideways, and here is what to do differently starting now.

The DISC is also one of the most valuable tools a leadership team can go through together. When you understand how your people are wired, you stop managing everyone the same way and start actually reaching them.

What is included
  • Full DISC behavioral assessment for you and your team
  • One-on-one or team debrief session walking through what the results mean in your specific context
  • Practical application to communication, conflict resolution, and leadership style
  • A reference tool your team can use long after the debrief is done
  • Can be added as a standalone session or built into any coaching or mastermind program
