The Mission

Give Them
the Blueprint.

Project Upward Path is a community-funded initiative to deliver adapted and original editions of Napoleon Hill's Think and Grow Rich to every child aged 8 to 18 in Horry County. Our mission is simple: equip the next generation with the principles of personal achievement, financial literacy, and ethical leadership, before the world tells them those lessons are not for them.

The Problem We're Solving

Growth at the Top
Doesn't Always Reach Down.

The Grand Strand is one of the fastest-growing regions in America. But too many Horry County kids are growing up without exposure to financial literacy, goal-setting, or the mindset tools that create long-term success.

The books that changed the lives of some of the most successful people in history are sitting on shelves these kids may never visit. We are changing that. One book at a time.

How It Works

Two Editions.
One County.

Ages 8 to 12
Think and Grow Rich for Kids

An adaptation that makes the core principles accessible and engaging for younger readers. Every book comes with a discussion guide for teachers, requiring zero extra prep.

Ages 13 to 18
Think and Grow Rich (Original)

The same unedited edition that has guided entrepreneurs, executives, and leaders for nearly a century. Spanish-language editions available for ESL students.

At the end of the year, student essay winners pitch how they applied the principles to a real idea, with seed-money grants on the line.
